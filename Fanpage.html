Logic Tribute Page

About

Sir Robert Bryson Hall II, professionally known as Logic/Young Sinatra, was raised in Gaithersburg, Maryland. Logic released his first mixtape in 2009 titled Logic: The Mixtape and another the following year titled Young, Broke, & Infamous. He signed with Visionary Music Group shortly thereafter. His fourth mixtape, Young Sinatra: Welcome to Forever debuted in 2013. This mixtape ultimately resulted in Logic signing with Def Jam Recordings. His 2017 album Everybody etched his claim to fame in stone on an international level. This was his first certified platinum album boasting the top-ten international hit "1-800-273-8255". That said, his platinum album deviated stylistically from the raw lyricism used to secure his initial fan base. This was rectified upon the release of his 2018 album YSIV (Young Sinatra IV) in which he returned to his lyricist roots. In addition to being a talented artist, Logic is also an accomplished author, having two published novels under his belt.
